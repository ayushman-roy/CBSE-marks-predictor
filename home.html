Class 12 Boards Marks Predictor


How It Works?

Enter your marks in the following form and click submit to get your predicted scores for Class 12 Boards Examinations 2021.
Make sure you enter valid marks only. If some marks are unavailable, please enter your best possible guess.
Only enter the theory marks of your best three subjects in Class 10. In case the maximum marks for such were not 80, please convert it to be upon 80 before submitting.
Formula for conversion :
(MARKS OBTAINED ⁄ MAXIMUM MARKS) X 80
Marks Distribution refers to the course marking structure. In case the maxmium practical marks in the subject was 30, please select the 70:30 option, and if the maximum practical marks in the subject was 20, please select the 80:20 option.
Only enter theory marks for all subjects in class 11 and 12 fields. Practical marks section refers to the marks obtained during the 2021 boards practical examinations.
Only enter the marks of your final examinations in class 11 section. For class 12, enter the marks obtained in the preboards or mid-term examinations, whichever was higher.
